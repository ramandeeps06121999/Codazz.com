## Image Optimization Checklist

Before using images:

- [ ] Download from Unsplash
- [ ] Compress to 100-200KB (use TinyPNG or Squoosh)
- [ ] Resize to appropriate dimensions:
  - Hero: 1920x1080 or 1200x630
  - Content: 800x600 or 1200x800
- [ ] Convert to WebP format (with JPEG fallback)
- [ ] Add descriptive alt text
- [ ] Credit photographer (required by Unsplash)
- [ ] Add to your CDN or hosting
